Avurudu Celebrations

Avurudu, also known as Sinhala and Tamil New Year, is an important cultural festival celebrated by the Sinhalese and Tamil communities in Sri Lanka. It marks the beginning of the new year according to the traditional Sinhala and Tamil calendars. The festival usually falls on April 13th or 14th each year, coinciding with the astrological transition of the sun from Pisces to Aries. Avurudu is a time for family gatherings, traditional rituals, delicious food, and various festive activities.
